Mashmellow

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, mix together the gelatin and cold water. Set aside to soften.

step 2

In saucepan, combine the granulated sugar, corn syrup, salt, and hot water. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar has dissolved.

step 3

Once the sugar has dissolved, stop stirring and attach a candy thermometer to the side of the pan. Increase the heat to high and continue to cook the mixture until it reaches 115Â°C.

step 4

Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the softened gelatin mixture until it has completely dissolved.

step 5

Using a stand mixer or hand mixer, beat the mixture on high speed for 10-12 minutes, until it becomes light and fluffy.

step 6

Add the vanilla extract and beat for an additional 1-2 minutes.

step 7

Dust the top of the marshmallow with powdered sugar, and allow it to set for at least 4 hours, or overnight.

ingredients

30g Gelatin

250g Sugar

120 ml Hot water

Vanilla essence

Food color

water

Corn flour
