Pizza

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Add the flour into a boul and milk sugar and salt.

step 2

Add 1/4 cup of water to the instant yeast and let if rise.

step 3

Add the yeast mixture to the floor and mix it well.

step 4

Take out the door and knead it rise.

step 5

Rise for 1 hour.

step 6

Take a small amout of the dought and make it as a rotty.

ingredients

3 cups of all-purpose flour

1 tsp sugar

1/2 tsp salt

1 tsp instant yeast

3 tsp Dry milk powder

2 tsp cooking oil

1 cup of luke warm water

Pizza souce

2 cups of grated chees
