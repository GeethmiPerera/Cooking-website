Mini Chocolate Cupcakes

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.

step 2

In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.

step 3

In another bowl, beat the eggs with beater until they become light and fluffy. Add in the granulated sugar and beat until well combined.

step 4

Gradually mix in the dry ingredients to the milk and hot water.

step 5

Fill the cupcake liners with batter until they are about 2/3 full.

step 6

Bake for 10-12 minutes,after cupcakes are cooled, you can frost them with your favorite frosting and decorate them as desired

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

1/2 cup granulated sugar

1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

2 eggs

vanilla extract

1/2 cup milk

baking soda

1/2 cup warm water

vegetable oil
