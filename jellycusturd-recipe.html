Jellycusturd

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Dissolve the jelly powder in 1 cup of boiling water. Stir until the powder has completely dissolved.

step 2

Add 1 cup of cold water to the jelly mixture, and stir to combine. Then add the fruit cocktail to the jelly mixture, and stir gently.

step 3

Pour the jelly mixture into a large bowl or individual cups, and refrigerate until set.

step 4

In a saucepan, combine the milk, heavy cream, sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks. Whisk together until well combined.

step 5

Place the saucepan over medium heat, and stir constantly until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.

step 6

Remove the saucepan from the heat, and stir in the vanilla extract.

step 7

Allow the custard mixture to cool for a few minutes, then pour it over the set jelly mixture.

step 8

Refrigerate the pudding for at least 2 hours or until set.

step 9

Serve the jelly custard pudding chilled, and enjoy!

ingredients

1 package jelly powder

1 cup boiling water

1 can of fruit cocktail, drained

1 cup milk

1 cup skim milk

1 cup heavy cream

2 table spoons cornstarch

3 egg yolks

1 teaspoon vanilla extract
